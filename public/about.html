NOAH SOKOLOFF
About

Noah Sokoloff (1985) is an interdisciplinary artist who works with the body, nature, and classical materials to explore the humanity and beauty within acts of struggle. Sokoloff spent 13 years as a glass blower, spending formative years as an apprentice in Morano Italy. There he learned how to question form and internalize the essential body movement that glass demands. His work abandons the traditional object form and uses movement within landscape to find the humanity in the absurd. His work has been shown at Ethan Cohen Fine arts and at KUBE beacon NY.
